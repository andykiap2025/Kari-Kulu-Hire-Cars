Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a vehicle, you will need a valid driver's license, a form of identification (passport or national ID), and a security deposit. Corporate clients may need to provide additional documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you offer delivery and pickup services?

Yes, we offer vehicle delivery and pickup services within Port Moresby for an additional fee. Please contact us for more information and to arrange this service.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include basic insurance coverage, maintenance, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Fuel is not included and the vehicle should be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup.
