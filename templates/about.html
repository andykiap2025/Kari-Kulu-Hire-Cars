About Us

Our Story

Kari Kulu Hire Cars is a premier vehicle rental service based in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea. We specialize in providing reliable, well-maintained vehicles for both personal and corporate clients throughout Port Moresby and the Central Province.

Since our establishment, we have built a reputation for excellent customer service, competitive rates, and a diverse fleet of vehicles to meet all transportation needs. Whether you need a compact car for city driving, a 4WD for rugged terrain, or a bus for group transportation, we have the perfect vehicle for your requirements.

Our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation solutions that exceed our customers' expectations. We take pride in maintaining our vehicles to the highest standards and offering personalized service to ensure your complete satisfaction.
