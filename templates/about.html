About Kari Kulu Hire Cars

Your trusted vehicle rental service in Papua New Guinea

Our Story

Kari Kulu Hire Cars was established in 2010 with a vision to provide reliable, affordable, and well-maintained vehicles for both tourists and locals in Papua New Guinea. What started as a small family business with just three vehicles has now grown into one of the most trusted vehicle rental services in the country.

Our founder, Mr. Kari Kulu, recognized the need for a dependable vehicle rental service that understood the unique terrain and travel requirements of Papua New Guinea. With his extensive knowledge of the local roads and conditions, he built a company that specializes in providing vehicles that are perfectly suited for PNG's diverse landscapes.

Our Mission

At Kari Kulu Hire Cars, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable vehicle rental services that exceed our customers' expectations. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of vehicle quality and customer service.

  • To provide well-maintained vehicles that ensure safety and comfort
  • To offer competitive and transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • To deliver exceptional customer service at every touchpoint
  • To contribute positively to the local community and economy
